Eminem

Who is Eminem?

Eminem is a world famous American rapper, record producer, song writter, film producer and also an actor. He was born in October 17th 1972 at St.Joseph, Missouri, United States.He also known as the king of hip hop.He is now among the best selling music artists of all time. He won so many awards including 15 Grammy Awards, 17 Billboard Music Awards, 8 American Music Awards and an Academy Award.

However his early life was not so good. He face lot of problems since his childhood.Eminem's parents were in a band called Daddy Warbucks. Therefore began to worked at several jobs to earn money to help his mother to pay bills in the house. But his mother always ignore him and throw out from the house. As a result he didn't love his mother . Therefore he make his own name as Eminem which means "Every mother is nice except mine".

Career Life

In 1988, he went by the stage name MC Double M and formed his first group New Jacks with DJ Butter Fingers. In 1989, they later joined Bassmint Productions who later changed their name to Soul Intent in 1992 with rapper Proof and other childhood friends. Eminem also made his first music video appearance in 1992 in a song titled, "Do-Da-Dippity", by Champtown. Later in 1996, Eminem and Proof teamed up with four other rappers to form The Dirty Dozen (D12), who released their first album Devil's Night in 2001. Eminem captured with the law at age 20.He was arrested for shooting with a paintball gun to a person when driving. Eminem was soon signed to Jeff and Mark Bass's FBT Productions, and recorded his debut album Infinite for their independent Web Entertainment label. The album was a commercial failure upon its release in 1996. As a result he was struggle to raise his newborn daughter, Hailie Jade Scott Mathers, with little money. At that time, Eminem's house was robbed several times.Eminem cooked and washed dishes for minimum wage at restaurant at St. Clair Shores. His former boss described him as becoming a model employee, as he worked 60 hours a week for six months after his daughter,s birth. He was fired shortly before Christmas, and later said, "It was, like, five days before Christmas, which is Hailie's birthday. Because of his personal problems he tried to suicide several times.By March 1997 he was fired from Gilbert's Lodge for the last time and lived in his mother's mobile home with Kim and Hailie.

Eminem is the one the greatest hip hop artist of all time.In 2012 Eminem is the seventh biggest icon in hip pop music.
